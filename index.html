Our Educational Academy, Harmony Heights, is a co-educational establishment founded in 2015 by the Self Trust Foundation®. Guided by the visionary leadership of Mr. Rakesh Das (Chairperson), our institution is committed to delivering outstanding education with a holistic approach. We strive to instill a passion for excellence in students, fostering comprehensive learning experiences. Operating under the guidelines of the National Curriculum, we aim to nurture well-rounded individuals who excel in various aspects of life.

The school's curriculum is designed to foster creativity, critical thinking skills, and a love for learning in its students. The school offers a variety of programs and extracurricular activities to complement its academic curriculum, including sports, music, drama, and more. Additionally, Your School Vidhya Mandhira prides itself on its continuous support for its students, ensuring that they have access to the resources and guidance they need to succeed both academically and personally. The institution's goal is to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge they need to become successful and faithful servants, honest leaders, and morally and socially empathetic individuals. The students are provided with an environment that fosters intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and a passion for learning. The faculty is committed to help each student reach their full potential by providing them with individualized attention and support. Students are encouraged to develop a strong moral compass, to be socially responsible and empathetic to the needs of others.

Your School Vidhya Mandhira is a school that provides an exceptional atmosphere for children to learn and grow. The school's child-friendly environment is designed to make children feel comfortable and at home. The facilities at the school are top-notch, providing students with an opportunity to learn in a modern and well-equipped setting.

The school prides itself on its activity and value-based learning, which is designed to help students develop their skills and knowledge in a way that is engaging and fun. The curriculum is designed to be child-centric, which means that students are encouraged to take an active role in their learning and are given the support they need to succeed. The school's infrastructure is also of a high standard, providing students with the resources they need to excel in their studies.

The school has a team of highly qualified and experienced teachers who are dedicated to help students achieve their full potential. They use innovative teaching methods and modern technology to make learning engaging and effective. The school also has a wide range of extracurricular activities that allow students to explore their interests and develop their skills outside the classroom.

Overall, Your School Vidhya Mandhira is an excellent choice for students who are looking for high-quality education at an affordable price. With its focus on academic excellence and personal development, the school is committed to help students achieve their goals and succeed in life.
